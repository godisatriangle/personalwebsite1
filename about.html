Hi, I’m Sandy. I hold a BFA Honours in Visual Arts and Philosophy from York University and an MA in Contemporary Art Theory from Goldsmiths College, University of London.

I paint stuff and write things. The stuff I paint is mostly just whatever needs to manifest in a painting at any given time. The things I write are mostly about art, culture and philosophy, and includes all sorts of content from longer-form articles to short snippets of marketing copy. I also make art of other sorts, but for now I'm going to stick to the painting stuff, at least for this website. All of my work is informed by my research interests, which includes subversive modes of temporality, the effects of digital immortality on labour and time, Marxist-feminist concepts of work, sea creatures from the Cambrian, iridescence and probably a lot of other topics.

Oh, and I also design and make websites. Nothing super complicated (yet), mind, but I made this one so ¯\_(ツ)_/¯
